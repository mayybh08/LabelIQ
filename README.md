## ðŸ§  Problem

- Food ingredient labels are designed for regulatory compliance, not for human understanding.
They often contain long lists of chemical names and fragmented health information, which makes it difficult for consumers to make confident decisions at the moment of purchase.

- Existing food and health apps fail to solve this problem because they:
1. Show raw data instead of insights
2. Require users to manually search, filter, or configure preferences
3. Treat AI as a feature rather than the interface

- As a result, users experience confusion exactly when decisions matter most.

---

## ðŸ’¡ Solution
- LabelIQ is an AI-native ingredient decision co-pilot that removes all user prompting and delivers instant decision clarity.

- The user simply pastes a product name or ingredient list.
- LabelIQ automatically reasons and explains:
1. Whether the product is okay to consume
2. Why it matters
3. How confident the science is
4. What could happen with regular consumption

- No forms. No filters. No questions.
